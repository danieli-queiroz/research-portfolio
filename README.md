# Research Portfolio

Welcome to my research portfolio.

This repository contains articles, essays, and research projects exploring the intersection of Artificial Intelligence, technology, society, public policy, and human development.

My work focuses on understanding how emerging technologies shape social structures, influence human behavior, transform labor markets, and create new ethical and governance challenges. Through research and analysis, I aim to contribute to discussions about the responsible development of technology and its role in promoting social progress.

## Articles

### The Century's Mismatch: The Advancement of Artificial Intelligence and the Inertia of Social Debate

**Summary:**

An analysis of the growing gap between the rapid advancement of Artificial Intelligence and the slower pace of social, ethical, and regulatory discussions.

The article explores the potential impacts of automation on labor markets, social inequality, and public policy, arguing for a more proactive approach to technology governance.

📄 Status: Published
